What is CoinTok?

CoinTok is built on Bonk, which lets developers claim fees from their own coin. We take these fees and use them to buy TikTok ads. This creates a cycle where the coin never stops advertising itself.

How It Works

1

Bonk Fees Generated

Every time someone buys or sells CoinTok, fees are created on the Bonk platform

2

Developers Claim Fees

Bonk allows us (the developers) to claim these fees from our own coin

3

Fees Buy TikTok Ads

We use the claimed fees to buy TikTok ads that promote CoinTok to millions of people

4

Never-Ending Cycle

More people see the ads and buy CoinTok, creating more fees, which buys more ads forever
